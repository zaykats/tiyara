01

The Mark

The graphic element carries two simultaneous readings — both intentional. One shape, two identities fused.

READING ONE

The Horizon

At 35,000 ft the horizon visibly curves. The arc is that exact shape — gradating from night-sky blue on the left to sunrise amber on the right, as it looks from a cockpit approaching Casablanca. This is the literal meaning of Afaq (آفاق) made visual.

READING TWO

The Engine Intake

Looking into the front of a CFM56 turbofan (ATA Chapter 72 — the engine Tiyara diagnoses) you see an outer intake ring, an inner ring, and fan blades between them. The same geometry, intentionally. The domain and the name, fused in one mark.

02

The Name

Afaq is not a brand invention — it is a real Arabic and Darija word with roots in everyday Moroccan language.

آفاق
Classical Arabic — plural of ufuq (أُفُق), meaning a single horizon. Afaq = many horizons — the full 360° view from altitude.
AFAQ
Moroccan Darija everyday word for horizon and future. "L-mustaqbal w-l-afaq" — the future and the horizons — is a common expression.
LANGUAGE
Classical Arabic and Moroccan Darija. Understood across all Arabic-speaking regions without translation.
WHY THIS WORD
First — aviation is the literal domain of horizons. Second — the document is about where Tiyara is going. Third — it ties the product to its Moroccan origin without needing an explanation.
RELATIONSHIP
Tiyara (طيارة = aircraft) is the product. Afaq is the vision document series inside it.
PRONUNCIATION
AH-faq  ·  آفاق — stress on the first syllable. Clear in Arabic, French, and English.

07

What Not To Do

remove the arc mark
AFAQ
Text alone is not the logo. The arc is not optional — it is half the meaning.
stretch the arc
The arc is a precise circle. Non-uniform scaling destroys its relationship to both the horizon and the engine ring.
recolor the gradient
Sky blue → Atlas amber is the only permitted gradient. It is derived from a specific moment, not a decorative choice.
replace the arc with an icon
AFAQ
No circles, crosses, planes, or icons. The arc is the only graphic element this identity uses.
use a different typeface
AFAQ
The wordmark is inseparable from Space Grotesk. Any other typeface is a different identity.
remove the Arabic script
AFAQ
The Arabic is not decoration. It is where the word comes from. Removing it strips the identity of its origin.
AFAQ
آفاق

This identity is part of the Tiyara brand system. It should be used exclusively for the innovation, vision, and strategy document series.
