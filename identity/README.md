# Afaq — Brand Identity

This folder contains the complete brand identity system for **Afaq (آفاق)**, the innovation and vision document series of Tiyara.

## What is in here

`index.html` — open this in any browser. It is a self-contained brand identity deck covering:

1. **The Name** — origin, meaning, why this word
2. **Color System** — palette rationale, the horizon gradient
3. **Typography** — Space Grotesk / Space Mono / Noto Sans Arabic / Inter and their roles
4. **Logo System** — anatomy, clearspace, logo on every background
5. **In Use** — document cover mockups, PDF stamp variant
6. **Misuse** — six things never to do

## Why Afaq

Afaq (آفاق) is the Moroccan Darija and Classical Arabic word for horizons — plural of ufuq (أُفُق). It is used daily in Morocco for both the literal horizon and the future. It works for three reasons: aviation is the domain of horizons, the document is about where Tiyara is going, and it ties the product to its Moroccan origin without needing a translation.

## The one rule

The horizon gradient line — sky blue fading into Atlas amber — is the single graphic element of this identity. It appears only as a 1.5px line. It is never used as a fill, a background, or applied to the letterforms of the wordmark.
